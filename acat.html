In 2019, Fjord, Intel & The Scott-Morgan Foundation redesigned ACAT, an assistive toolkit for MND patients, originally created for Stephen Hawking. This project aimed to allow sufferers to retain those most human of traits: communication and personality, by leveraging the bleeding edge technologies like speech-bank based voice synthesis, AI predictive text and 3D avatars.

We worked closely with super-scientist Dr. Peter Scott-Morgan, who was diagnosed with MND in 2017. Peter is harnessing technology to help him ‚Äì and other people with extreme disabilities ‚Äì live an enhanced life.

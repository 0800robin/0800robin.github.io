uFab is powerful quoting platform which transforms the machining cost estimation process. https://www.ufab.io/

I spent the summer of 2018 at Fjord Stockholm 🇸🇪 working as a front-end developer on uFab. We worked in an agile 8~ person SCRUM to design and deliver uFab in collaboration with a global team.

My main role was implementing components in React and handling app state with Redux. I also worked on displaying 3D part models in three.js and extending the Ant Design component library. I thoroughly enjoyed being a bridge between the design and engineering team.

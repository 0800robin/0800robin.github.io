uFab is powerful quoting platform which transforms the machining cost estimation process.

I spent the summer of 2018 at Fjord Stockholm working as a front-end developer on uFab. We worked in an agile 8~ person SCRUM to design and deliver uFab in collaboration with a global team.

My role was focused on implementing components in React, displaying 3D models in three.js, keeping things in check with Redux and a lot of CSS to extend the Ant Design component library. I thoroughly enjoyed being a bridge between the UI design and Engineering team.

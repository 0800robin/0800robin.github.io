Burberry Harrods Takeover

A Burberry-branded AI Photobooth + Roblox world for the 2024 Harrods takeover.

Press Release • Burberry on Roblox

For Burberry's takeover of Harrods in 2024, the Creative Tech team were tasked with creating an in-store interactive experience and a way of bringing the collaboration to life for people who couldn't access the physical location. We produced an AI photobooth for customers to take branded selfies on in Harrods. We also used our extensive knowledge and prototypes of always-on-gaming platforms to collaborate with a 3rd party on the creation of a branded Burberry x Harrods world on Roblox.

I led the development of the photobooth experience from prototyping through to live deployment, focusing on reliability, speed, and polish under real-world conditions.. Using real-time AI background AIsegmentation, the booth allowed customers to take selfies on a Burberry Harrods Check pattern background. 4 images were transformed into looping GIFs and emailed to the customer.

Adventures In 10 Print is an interactive generative art work and gallery exploring the themes of Chance and Control.

Project Overview üåç

In September 2020, I was commissioned to explore the theme of 'Chance And Control' by the V&A and Chester Visual Arts.

I built Adventures In 10 Print, an interactive website enabling people of all technical abilities to play around the classic computer art algorithm and share what they generate to a public gallery.

About üé®

The 10 Print algorithm is the essence of 'Chance and Control'. Each time it runs, the computer generated randomness produces an entirely new pattern, while retaining a fundamental set of aesthetic characteristics the viewer can control.

Creating a little spot in cyberspace which lets people of all technical abilities create and share algorithmic presents an interesting dynamic. The computer screen is both medium of creation and display & re-appropriates the core languages of the web (HTML/CSS/JS) to serve artistic ends in a fun and playful way.

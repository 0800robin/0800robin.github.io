Ocean is a fun and simple way to make music with friends using just your web browser. https://www.oceanwaves.io

During my post-graduate year at Goldsmiths I researched how people work collaborate online. I explored how different roles naturally emerge when working remotely together & the technical and UX approaches to successfully design systems which fun, engaging web based collaboration.

Ocean has been iterated on many times over a number of years as a beloved personal project. I am the sole developer & work in collaboration with two friends who work in music community & UX design. Our aim is to build a fun and simple way to make music and break down the barriers associated with electronic music production.

During the 2020 lockdown, Ocean has attracted a global user base, from Wuhan to Damascus. During a time where friends and normal life feel very distant, seeing other people expressing themselves & being 'along together' using a tool i've created has been incredibly rewarding.

I'd love to talk more one to one about how Ocean was built, where its heading and the state of audio on the web!

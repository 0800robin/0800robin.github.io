Ocean

A fun and simple way to make music with friends using just your web browser.

This project is an exploration into interaction & technological approaches to music making and how the web as a platform is evolving into a powerful medium for creative, interactive apps.

During my post-graduate research at Goldsmiths i explored how people work together on the web, the roles that emerge and the UX and technological considerations that promote fun remote collaboration.

Live in beta https://www.oceanwaves.io
